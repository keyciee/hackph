Programs · Mga Programa

For every
Filipino hacker

Hack PH programmes are built for the Philippine context — from provincial universities with limited resources, to world-class researchers competing at DEF CON, to the vast OFW community doing security work overseas.

Community

PWNPH — National CTF

The Philippines' premier Capture The Flag competition — open to all skill levels, from Luzon to Mindanao, and to Filipinos in the diaspora worldwide.

Education

Provincial Hacker Labs

Mobile training labs bringing cybersecurity education to Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities — because talent doesn't only live in Metro Manila.

Industry

BPO Security Upskill

Cybersecurity training designed for the 1.3 million Filipinos working in BPO and shared services — the sector most exposed to social engineering and data attacks.

Diaspora

OFW Tech Network

Connecting Filipino security professionals working abroad — in Singapore, UAE, the US, Australia — with mentorship opportunities back home.

Research

PH Bug Bounty Community

A structured community around bug bounty hunting — helping Filipino researchers turn their skills into income on global platforms like HackerOne and Bugcrowd.

Enterprise

PH Cyber Talent Pipeline

Connecting vetted Hack PH community members with cybersecurity roles at Philippine banks, telcos, government agencies, and multinational companies.
